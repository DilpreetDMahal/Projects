3-2 Argentina comebacks from a two goals deficit againt Egypt

Today, the current world champions met Mohamed Salah's Egypt in a Round of 16 match in Atlanta. The match began with Egypt scoring in the first 15 minutes and then scoring again in the second half. Afterwards, Romero scored a header, Messi equalised, and finally, Enzo Fernández scored in added time. Argentina snatched the victory in the dying minutes.

  • Argentine met Egypt after 18 years.
  • Messi became the highest scorer in World Cup history with 21 goals
  • Enzo Fernandez's goal breaks Egypt's heart.
